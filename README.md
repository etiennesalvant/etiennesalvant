### 💻 What I do

I'm a frontend developer at **SLG Innovation**, a Chicago-based government IT consulting firm, where I own most of the web development for our public site and internal tooling — everything from site architecture to animation systems to form pipelines.

Outside of that, I build and ship commercial web products on my own:

- 🛍️ **StoreBeat** — a three-tier beat store template suite for audio producers (Shopify/Next.js, WordPress/WooCommerce, and standalone HTML versions), with a shared neon-green-on-dark design system.
- 🌊 **Wave Dancewear** — a custom Shopify OS 2.0 theme build (brand: Tsunami/Noelle Namiko), with configurable collection templates, cursor particle trails, parallax scrolling, and 3D card tilt effects.
- 🌃 **The Happening** — a local nightlife/event discovery app with multi-city data, a sports tab powered by ESPN's API, and Google Places integration.
- 🚗 **Rev-Tech Pro** — a full-stack automotive e-commerce template (Next.js 14, TypeScript, Tailwind, PostgreSQL, Stripe) with a guided setup wizard built in.
- 📦 **Wares.io** — a single-file React/Vite inventory management app with a storefront, barcode scanning, CSV import, and live dashboards.

I like shipping **complete, working products** — not scaffolds or half-built demos.

### 🎵 What I make

When I'm not writing code, I'm writing songs. I make atmospheric R&B and drill, pulling influence from artists like **Tink** and **Doja Cat**. I play guitar, write most of my own material, and I'm actively building up my vocal and keyboard chops.
